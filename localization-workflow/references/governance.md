# Governance Reference

## What Can Proceed Automatically

1. hardcoded extraction
2. key alignment
3. ordering and grouping normalization
4. obvious mixed-language cleanup
5. high-confidence visible-copy cleanup
6. terminology consistency cleanup

## What Should Trigger Review

1. brand voice ambiguity
2. paywall conversion tone tradeoffs
3. concept-heavy astrology terminology that may shift meaning
4. final release acceptance

## Collaboration Policy

1. Prefer coherent batches over endless micro-edits.
2. Prefer one module or one content family at a time.
3. Summarize only at useful checkpoints.
