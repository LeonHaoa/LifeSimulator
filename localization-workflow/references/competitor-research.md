# Competitor Research Reference

## Purpose

Use competitor research to choose tone and copy direction, not to clone wording.

## When to Use

1. when product tone is not yet frozen
2. when paywall style is unclear
3. when onboarding or daily content tone needs reference points

## Suggested Output

1. product tone summary
2. CTA style summary
3. onboarding style summary
4. what to borrow
5. what to avoid

## Research Guardrails

1. compare tone, rhythm, clarity, and restraint
2. avoid direct wording reuse
3. prefer product-fit over trend imitation
